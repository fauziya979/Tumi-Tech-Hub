About Us

WHO WE ARE

Spring Minds is a dedicated organization committed to supporting children with learning differences such as dyslexia and autism. We believe that every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and succeed in an environment that understands their unique needs. The awareness and support for neurodiverse children vary across societies and educational systems. Spring Minds works to bridge these differences through mentorship, inclusive programs, and community education, helping children and families find hope, confidence, and the right path to learning success.

Awareness and support

In school, most subject matter knowledge is accessed by reading and mostly communicated through writing. As such, a student with dyslexia feels that the school world is barricaded by the locked gate of numerous texts. However, with the appropriate classroom accommodations, they are able to access the same instructional materials whilst meeting academic expectations as their peers in class.
