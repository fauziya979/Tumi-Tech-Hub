Supporting Reading Development at Home

Dr. Lisa Thompson November 18, 2025 4 min read
Reading is a fundamental skill that opens doors to learning and imagination. Supporting your child's reading development at home creates a strong foundation for academic success and lifelong learning.

Building Reading Foundations

Early reading skills develop through exposure to language, books, and storytelling. Create a rich literacy environment that encourages exploration and curiosity about words and stories.

Reading Together

Daily reading time, even just 15-20 minutes, significantly impacts reading development. Choose books that match your child's interests and reading level.

Effective Strategies

  • Read aloud regularly to model fluent reading
  • Ask questions about stories to build comprehension
  • Encourage your child to retell favorite stories
  • Create opportunities for writing and drawing

Making Reading Enjoyable

The goal is to foster a love of reading that will last a lifetime. Let children choose books that interest them, create cozy reading spaces, and celebrate reading achievements.
